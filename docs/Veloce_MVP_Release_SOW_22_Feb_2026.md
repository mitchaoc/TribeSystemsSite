
# TRIBE and Veloce 

## Statement of Work (SOW) – MVP Launch 2026

**Version:** 1.0

**Date:** February 2026

**Related Document:** TRIBE x Veloce Launch Roadmap (v2)

---

# 1. Purpose

This Statement of Work defines:

- The agreed MVP scope for UK launch (June 2026)
- Responsibilities of each party
- Excluded functionality
- Commercial dependencies
- Delivery milestones

This SOW governs Phase 1 (Build), Phase 2 (Pilot), and Phase 3 (Public Launch).

Any features not expressly included are considered out of scope.

---

# 2. Commercial Model Context

The parties acknowledge:

- Tribe receives revenue primarily from customer subscriptions and interchange.
- Veloce receives marketing support funding from the card scheme based on active cardholders.
- Growth and activation are shared objectives.
- Neither party is paying an upfront enterprise software fee.

Accordingly, scope discipline and launch velocity are mutually critical.

---

# 3. MVP Scope (Included)

## 3.1 Core Banking

Tribe will deliver:

- UK onboarding + KYC (via sponsor bank)
- Wallet-first home screen
- Virtual debit card issuance
- Real-time transaction history
- Card controls (freeze, limits, view details)
- Add to Apple / Google Wallet
- Physical card ordering (£4.99)
- Top-ups (card, digital wallet, bank transfer)
- Outbound UK bank transfers (Faster Payments)
- Account settings & profile
- Push notifications
- Tier 1 AI-assisted support
- App Store & Play Store deployment

---

## 3.2 Rewards & Commerce Layer

Tribe will deliver:

- Dedicated Rewards tab
- Tier display (Everyday / Plus / Premium)
- Basic referral tracking
- External Shop linking capability

Veloce will provide:

- Reward inventory (merch, events, experiences, partner offers)
- Fulfilment responsibility for non-financial rewards
- Reward funding budget (see Section 6)

---

## 3.3 Ecosystem MVP (Lightweight Engagement Layer)

### A. Ecosystem Highlights Module

A dynamic carousel (max 1–3 items) displayed below the wallet card.

Capabilities:

- Configurable highlight cards
- Click tracking
- Impression tracking
- Deep linking to rewards or offers

No editorial feed included at launch.

---

### B. Engagement Rewards Engine

Behaviour-triggered incentives including:

- Spend thresholds
- Referral milestones
- Activation rewards
- Event-linked boosts

Reward logic configurable by Tribe.

Financial backing required from Veloce (see Section 6).

---

# 4. Explicitly Out of Scope (Launch)

The following are NOT included in MVP:

- P2P payments
- Request money
- Standing orders
- Direct debits
- Savings pots
- Community chat or social features
- Editorial news feed
- User-generated content
- Marketplace functionality
- Crypto features
- Advanced AI personalisation
- Open banking integrations

These may be evaluated post-launch subject to milestone achievement.

---

# 5. Delivery Phases

## Phase 1 – Build (Q1 2026)

- Final wireframe approval
- Backend infrastructure setup
- Sponsor bank integration
- Wallet build
- Rewards framework
- Analytics instrumentation

## Phase 2 – Pilot (Apr–May 2026)

- Limited invite cohort
- Stability validation
- Activation optimisation
- Engagement tracking validation
- Support process refinement

No major scope expansion during pilot.

## Phase 3 – Public Launch (June 2026)

- App Store release
- Coordinated marketing push
- Scheme reporting alignment
- Growth monitoring

---

# 6. Dependencies & Financial Commitments

For Engagement Rewards to function, Veloce agrees to:

- Allocate a defined rewards budget
- Confirm reward funding structure prior to build completion
- Provide reward inventory before pilot launch

If no funding is confirmed, the Engagement Rewards engine will be disabled at launch.

---

# 7. Responsibilities

## Tribe Responsibilities

- Product design & UX
- Engineering & infrastructure
- Banking integration
- Compliance coordination with sponsor bank
- Analytics & reporting infrastructure
- AI support configuration
- Ongoing platform stability

## Veloce Responsibilities

- Brand assets
- Marketing distribution
- Reward fulfilment
- Reward funding
- Campaign approvals
- Card scheme coordination

---

# 8. Change Management

Any requested feature not listed in Section 3:

- Will be assessed for delivery impact
- May alter launch timeline
- May require reprioritisation of existing scope

Material scope expansion may require formal amendment to this SOW.

---

# 9. Success Metrics (Shared)

Launch success indicators include:

- Onboarding completion rate
- Card activation rate (within 7 days)
- Monthly transacting users
- Engagement Highlight interaction rate
- Reward redemption rate
- App store rating > 4.2

Growth milestone:

- 25,000+ monthly transacting users
