# Product Roadmap – TRIBE / E bank / XX Group
This roadmap reflects the revised delivery plan aligned to a first scaled public launch at
Money 2026 (June), with a Family & Friends pilot in April/May 2026 and a reduced MVP
scope for initial release to keep to original timelines. Subsequent phases expand
monetisation, engagement, and advanced capabilities through 2nd half of 2026 and 2027.

## Key Assumptions
• Launch market: UK-only for June 2026; EU expansion post-launch
• Cards: Virtual-first at launch; physical cards TBC
• Customer support: TRIBE-led Tier 1 support with AI automation; E Bank managing
disputes
• Sponsor monetisation: Deferred to post-launch phases
• Crypto /NFTs: Deferred to post-launch phases

## Phase 0 – Scope Lock & Programme Reset (Jan 2026)
• Lock MVP feature scope aligned to June 2026 launch
• Confirm compliance, operational and support models between TRIBE / E Bank
• Confirm marketing plan, tiers, branding & customer incentives between TRIBE / XX Group
• Finalise Figma designs for MVP June 2026 launch
• Define launch success metrics and go/no-go criteria

## Phase 1 – Continue Core Product Build (Q1 2026)
• User onboarding and KYC (UK)
• Account creation and virtual card issuance
• Card controls (freeze/unfreeze, view details)
• Transaction history (MVP)
• Top-ups and card payments
• Branded content feed (images & video)
• Push notifications
• Foundational data tracking and consent framework (AI learning built in from start)
• Customer support AI interface

### Out of Scope for MVP
• P2P payments
• Community or social features
• Creator commerce or marketplaces
• Crypto, tokens or Web3 features
• Advanced analytics or spend insights
• User-facing AI beyond support automation
• 3DS payments (approve on-line payments)

## Phase 2 – Family & Friends Pilot (Apr–May 2026)
• Invite-only pilot (100–500 users)
• Validate onboarding, card spend, content delivery and support flows
• AI-powered Tier 1 customer support live
• Manual monitoring and incident management
• Bug fixing and UX polish only via customer feedback (no new major features)

## Phase 3 – Public Launch @ Event X 2026 (June 2026)
• UK public launch on iOS and Android
• Virtual debit card live at scale
• Core banking and content experience
• Basic sponsor visibility (non-monetised)
• PR and industry launch aligned to Money 2026

## Phase 4 – Monetisation & Engagement Expansion (Q3–Q4 2026)
• Loyalty and rewards engine
• Sponsor-funded offers and campaigns
• Personalised content and offers (AI-assisted)
• Enhanced analytics and reporting for partners
• EU market rollout/preparation
• P2P Payments
• Community & Social features (MVP)
• Creator commerce (MVP)
• More features/advanced versions of MVP scope based on user feedback

## Phase 5 – Ecosystem Expansion (2027)
• Crypto and stablecoin features via third-party partners (including VEXT, Bitcoin,
Stablecoin, etc)
• Token-gated fan experiences
• Creator payouts and advanced engagement mechanics
• Marketplace and premium subscriptions
• Community & Social features (advanced)
• Creator commerce (MVP)
